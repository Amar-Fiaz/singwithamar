Vocal Warm-ups

Warming up your voice is crucial for a good performance. Try these exercises:

    Hum gently to relax your vocal cords.

    Do lip trills to improve breath control.

    Sing scales to warm up your vocal range.

Singing Tips

Enhance your singing skills with these helpful tips:

    Maintain good posture for better breath support.

    Practice deep breathing to control your voice.

    Stay hydrated to keep your vocal cords healthy.

    Listen to various styles to expand your range.
